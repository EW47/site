How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

  1. First you go to portal.hz.nl and log in with your account.
  2. After that you click on "Change personal menu".
  3. Add "Selfservice Portal".
  4. Save your changes and go to the page.
  5. Click on "Make a reservation".
  6. Then click on "Book project groups rooms".
  7. Pick a time and place that ins't reserved already and you're done!
