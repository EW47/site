How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

  1. First you go to print.hz.nl and log in with your account.
  2. After that you choose the file you want to print.
  3. Click on "Next".
  4. Select the printer that best suits your document.
  5. Go to the printer that you've selected and you're done!
