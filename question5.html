What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

  1. Free parking is available at the car park of PZEM at Poelendaelesingel 10 in Middelburg.
  2. At the barrier of the car park the Poelendaelesingel you can ring the bell.
  3. After passing the barrier, follow the signs to the HZ parking places, marked with a white sign with the HZ logo.
  4. Students and staff can only enter by presenting their HZ pass at the barriers, guests can ring the bell at the barrier.
